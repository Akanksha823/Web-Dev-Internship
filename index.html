Stepping into the 25th Year of its existence and excellence in transformational education, Manav Rachna has been recently graced as the only university in north India to be adorned with three national awards by the President of India. Additionally, it has been conferred with the impressive 118th rank by NIRF.

Manav Rachna Educational Institutions encompasses a group of vibrant, world-class institutions offering education from Pre-nursery to Doctoral level. From two universities: MRIIRS (a Deemed to be University which also includes Manav Rachna Dental College) and MRU (a State Private University); 8 Manav Rachna International Schools, and 4 Schools under the Kunskapsskolan banner (under a joint venture with our Swedish partner Kunskapsskolan Education), Manav Rachna strives to provide impetus to every learner interested to excel in life. The institutions have over 32,000+ alumni base, 500+ reputed MNCs & Indian Corporate patrons, 579 Patents (filed/granted), 56+ global academic collaborations, 80+ Alumni & In Campus start-ups, 13 are the countries from which Manav Rachna students arrive, and 7305 Research papers have been published in International / National Journals and Conferences.

With NAAC ‘A’ grade accreditation, 5 Star QS rating for Teaching, Employability, Academic Development, Facilities, Social Responsibility and Inclusiveness; a host of affiliated courses (UGC, AICTE, NAAC, NBA, NCTE, BCI etc), amazing placement statistics, impressive hostels, alluring infrastructure, greatly experienced faculty members, fun campus life, amazing student clubs, vibrant cafeterias, technology enabled massive library, prestigious rankings, industry-aligned programs, merit based scholarships, CCTV and security equipped campus, and state-of-the-art offerings; it is no wonder that students, researchers and industries alike flock towards Manav Rachna.
